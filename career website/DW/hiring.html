Who Hires Software Engineers?

Almost any company will hire a software engineer. Software engineers are obviously hired by technology based companies like Intel, Google, Microsoft, Dell, and Amazon. However, most companies are wanting to hire software engineers. Even NASA needs software engineers.

Software engineers can get creative with their hiring prospects. Even Hollywood needs software engineers to help animate their upcoming movies.

There aren't many limits to who will hire a software engineer.
